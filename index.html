What is PLASSX?

An AI visibility engine
for the new discovery layer.

PLASSX is an AI visibility engine that ensures businesses are clearly understood and recommended inside AI-generated answers.

As discovery shifts from search engines to AI assistants, platforms like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Perplexity no longer show users endless lists of results. They select a small number of businesses to recommend.

If a business is not selected, it becomes invisible at the moment of decision.

PLASSX analyzes how AI systems interpret a business and identifies what must be improved to increase its likelihood of being included in AI-generated recommendations.

Traditional search
Users browse, compare, and choose.
Businesses compete across search results, maps, directories, websites, reviews, and listings. The user still does much of the filtering.
AI discovery
AI systems interpret, filter, and recommend.
AI assistants synthesize available signals and present a smaller set of options. The businesses they understand best have a stronger chance of being selected.

Why AI visibility matters

Search behavior
is changing.

Users are no longer only browsing links, maps, or directories. They are asking AI systems for direct recommendations and acting on the answers.

This creates a new visibility reality: fewer businesses are shown, competition is compressed, recommendation slots become more valuable, and structured, AI-readable businesses gain an advantage.

Fewer businesses are shown
AI answers tend to compress discovery into a smaller set of suggested options rather than pages of results.
Competition is compressed
When users ask AI for a recommendation, being clearly understood can matter as much as being available online.
Recommendation slots become more valuable
Being included in the answer can influence customer choice earlier than a website visit, directory click, or ad impression.
AI-readable businesses gain an advantage
PLASSX helps businesses compete inside this new discovery layer by improving the signals AI systems rely on.

What is AI visibility?
AI visibility refers to whether a business is recognized, understood, and recommended by AI systems when users ask for suggestions.
