PRIVACY POLICY — PLASSX

This Privacy Policy explains how PLASSX collects, uses, processes, and protects information when you use the platform.

1. Introduction

PLASSX (“PLASSX”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is a predictive career intelligence platform designed to help users understand where professional opportunities may emerge and how to position themselves accordingly.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your information when you use our platform.

By using PLASSX, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account credentials
  • Resume/CV content
  • Professional experience
  • Skills, roles, and preferences
  • Career goals and targets
  • Any content you voluntarily submit

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Usage data (pages visited, actions taken)
  • Session behavior
  • Interaction with platform features

2.3 External Data Signals

PLASSX processes publicly available and third-party data sources, including:

  • Company announcements
  • Hiring signals
  • Business events (e.g., funding, expansion)
  • Public professional data

This data is used to generate insights and predictions and is not tied to individual users unless explicitly provided.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Analyze your professional profile
  • Generate opportunity predictions
  • Provide personalized recommendations
  • Improve platform accuracy
  • Deliver insights and guidance
  • Communicate updates and notifications
  • Ensure platform security and integrity

4. AI and Automated Processing

PLASSX uses algorithmic systems and artificial intelligence to:

  • Analyze user profiles
  • Detect market signals
  • Estimate hiring probabilities
  • Generate recommendations and guidance

These outputs are predictive and informational, not guaranteed outcomes.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure data storage systems
  • Access control and authentication
  • Monitoring for unauthorized access

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, infrastructure)
  • AI providers (for processing and generation tasks)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

All partners are expected to handle data securely and in compliance with applicable regulations.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services
  • Maintain account functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations

You may request deletion of your data at any time.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to certain uses
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@plassx.com

9. Cookies and Tracking

PLASSX may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Maintain session state

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with third-party tools and services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

11. Children’s Privacy

PLASSX is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes where appropriate.

13. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy:

Email: privacy@plassx.com
