Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20 Jan 2026

PLASSX ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard personal information when you visit our website, contact us, or engage with PLASSX in relation to our holding company and portfolio activities.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to individuals and organizations who:

  • Visit the PLASSX website
  • Submit inquiries through our website or contact channels
  • Communicate with PLASSX regarding strategic, investment, or partnership matters
  • Engage with PLASSX in connection with portfolio companies or business opportunities

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of data:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Organization or business affiliation, where provided
  • Phone number, if voluntarily submitted
  • Any inquiry details or supporting information you choose to share

2.2 Business Interaction Information

  • Correspondence records
  • Meeting or call notes
  • Information relevant to business development, partnership, or investment discussions

2.3 Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and general website usage information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries sent to PLASSX
  • To evaluate prospective business, investment, or partnership opportunities
  • To manage communications relating to PLASSX and its portfolio companies
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Confidentiality

PLASSX may receive confidential or commercially sensitive information in the course of communications.

Where appropriate, submitted information may be treated as confidential and handled with internal access controls consistent with the nature of the communication and applicable legal obligations.

We reserve the right to:

  • Limit access to information on a need-to-know basis
  • Maintain records relevant to business communications
  • Protect proprietary and commercially sensitive information

5. Data Sharing

PLASSX does not sell personal data.

We may share information only with:

  • Professional advisers, service providers, or consultants supporting our operations
  • Technical providers involved in website hosting, analytics, or communications
  • Portfolio companies or counterparties where relevant and appropriate to the inquiry
  • Legal, regulatory, or governmental authorities when required

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Access control mechanisms

No digital system can be guaranteed fully secure, and users acknowledge that residual risk remains.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • For as long as reasonably necessary for operational and business purposes
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To maintain records of relevant communications and business interactions

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)

Requests may be directed to contact@plassx.com.

9. International Data Processing

Your data may be processed in jurisdictions outside your country of residence where PLASSX, its advisers, or service providers operate. By engaging with PLASSX, you acknowledge that such cross-border processing may occur in accordance with applicable law.

10. Contact and Policy Updates

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be sent to contact@plassx.com.

PLASSX may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any revisions will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
