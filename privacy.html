Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20 Jan 2026

PLASSX (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and safeguard your information when you access our platform, website, or services.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users who:

  • Visit the PLASSX website

  • Engage with PLASSX services or communications

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of data:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Country of residence
  • Any information voluntarily submitted

2.2 Transaction Information

  • Payment confirmation data
  • Billing details (processed via third-party providers)
  • Access records

2.3 Technical Data

  • IP address

  • Device information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To process Private Access transactions
  • To grant and manage access to restricted PLASSX materials

  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Confidentiality of Access

PLASSX operates a controlled-access model.

Information disclosed after payment may be confidential, restricted, and subject to internal access controls.

We reserve the right to:

  • Restrict, limit, or revoke access
  • Monitor usage of restricted materials
  • Protect proprietary infrastructure data

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell personal data.

We may share information only with:

  • Payment processors (for transaction handling)
  • Technical service providers (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal or regulatory authorities if required

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Access control mechanisms

However, no system is completely secure, and users acknowledge this risk.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as necessary for operational purposes

  • To maintain records of access and transactions

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)

Requests can be submitted via official PLASSX contact channels.

9. International Data Processing

Your data may be processed in jurisdictions different from your country of residence. By using PLASSX, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to Policy

PLASSX reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
